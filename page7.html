INFOGRAPHIC LEAFLET

CREATING A INFOGRAPHIC LEAFLET

First I designed a base layer of abstract multicoloured shapes to attract the views eye, grabbing their attention. Then, to create depth in my leaflet I imported images of Winchester some taken from the internet some taken by me personally to display the beautiful scenery around Winchester. Adding subheadings helps direct the viewers to the content that they want to read.
