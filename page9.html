ACADEMIC POSTER


designing an academic poster

1)At the start of my designing academic poster i created 2 ergonomic shapes and 2 bubbles to mold my text and images around, and created a layer of depth from the blue accents of the image appearing to be on/infront of the white background.

2) Next creating more texture to the image by filling the white background with stock images that represent me personally on some level and using diagonal stripes on the blue areas to make the image more intresting.

3) Adding all the text to inform the viewer about myself and moulding the text to the surrounding areas to make it feel cohesive and comfortable in the image.

4) Finally, adding back and white pictures of myself so the audience can connect more personally with me, and adding final touches to the image to refine it.
